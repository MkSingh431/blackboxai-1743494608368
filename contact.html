Frequently Asked Questions

Download the Uber app from the App Store or Google Play, then follow the prompts to sign up with your email address and phone number. You'll need to add a payment method before you can request your first ride.

Visit our driver signup page and complete the application process. You'll need a valid driver's license, proof of vehicle insurance, and to meet the minimum age requirement in your city. Background and vehicle checks are also required.

Uber uses dynamic pricing that takes into account the distance of your trip, local traffic conditions, and current demand for rides. You'll always see the price upfront before confirming your ride. Prices may be higher during busy times.

If you have an immediate safety concern, please contact local authorities. You can also report safety issues through the Uber app by going to your trip history, selecting the relevant trip, and choosing "I had a safety issue." Our safety team will follow up.

If you believe you're entitled to a refund, go to your trip history in the Uber app, select the trip in question, and choose "Review my fare or fees." From there you can submit a refund request. Our support team will review your request and respond.
