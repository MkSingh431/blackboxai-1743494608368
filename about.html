Our Story

Founded in 2009, Uber started as a simple idea: What if you could request a ride from your phone? Today, we've grown into a global platform powering smarter transportation for people, food, and things across 10,000+ cities.

Our technology creates new possibilities for people to move, earn, and work in ways that were never before possible.

Our Journey

2009

Uber is founded in San Francisco as UberCab by Garrett Camp and Travis Kalanick

2011

Expanded internationally to Paris, our first city outside North America

2014

Launched UberEATS (now Uber Eats) food delivery service

2017

Introduced Uber Freight to transform the trucking industry

2023

Operating in 10,000+ cities with over 130 million monthly active users
